Help for VPN Application

What is a VPN?

A Virtual Private Network (VPN) is a service that creates a secure connection over the internet between your device and a VPN server. It encrypts your internet traffic and hides your IP address, providing privacy and security while browsing online.

How to Connect

  1. Open the VPN application.
  2. Select a server from the list of available servers.
  3. Click on the "Connect" button.
  4. Once connected, you will see a confirmation message.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues while using the VPN, please try the following:

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Try connecting to a different server.
  • Restart the VPN application.
  • Make sure your firewall or antivirus is not blocking the VPN connection.
